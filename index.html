I'm Sarveshwar Singh — a software engineer and entrepreneur based in Tokyo, Japan. With over 15 years in the industry, I specialise in building decentralised systems, blockchain infrastructure, and enterprise software across retail, HR, and real estate sectors.

Deep in the Ethereum ecosystem since its early days, I work with Cardano, NFTs, IPFS, and smart contract platforms. I believe the most important software isn't just technically sound — it solves real human problems.

My focus is on solutions that remove friction — moving systems from trust-based to trustless. I'm drawn to the digitization and tokenization of real-world assets, decentralized digital identity (DID), and public-good infrastructure — especially governance and civic-tech projects built with governments like India, Japan, and beyond.
